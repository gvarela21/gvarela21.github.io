About Me

Started my education at Orange Coast College where I took course work in Photography, some Fashion Design, and even Sailing before getting stuck in the work grind.

Ten years of experience working as a Handy Woman in commercial/ residential repairs, remodels, and installations.

Almost a year of experience as a Cable Technician with Spectrum(formally Time Warner Cable) climbing Telephone Poles for residential internet and cable installations.

Decided to make a career change and finished my Associates in Arts for transfer in Southern California.

Moved to the Bay Area to Continue my education at Cal State University East Bay.

In my free time I enjoy Kayaking in Alameda with my dog and exploring the bay area by motorcycle.
